Delete Your Account

Last updated: June 2026  ·  tarati.tech

Tarati is a strategy board game developed by Agustín Gomila. This page explains how to request deletion of your Tarati account and the data associated with it.

Delete from within the app (recommended)

You can delete your account at any time, directly from any platform (Android, Desktop or Web):

  1. Open Settings.
  2. Tap the Online section and select your username.
  3. In Online settings, scroll to the Danger zone.
  4. Tap Delete account and confirm.

Deletion is immediate and permanent. No further action is required.

Delete by email request

If you can no longer access the app, send an email from the address associated with your account to tarati.gameboard@gmail.com with the subject "Tarati account deletion request" and include your Tarati username. We will process the request and confirm within 30 days.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed from our servers immediately (cascade delete):

Data Status on deletion
Account credentials (username, email, password hash) Deleted
Profile data (display name, country, bio, preferences) Deleted
Ratings, statistics and achievements Deleted
Game history and saved games Deleted
Social data (follows, followers, challenges) Deleted
Active sessions and matchmaking entries Deleted

What data is retained

We do not retain personal data after deletion. Transient operational records may persist briefly:

  • IP address: used only temporarily for authentication rate-limiting; not stored persistently.
  • Server logs: standard request logs may exist for up to 30 days for security and abuse prevention, then are rotated out automatically. These do not constitute a usable profile.

Contact

Questions about deletion or your data: tarati.gameboard@gmail.com
